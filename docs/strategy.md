# JoCo Home Pros — SEO, Monetization & Growth Strategy

## What This Site Is Trying to Accomplish

JoCo Home Pros is a **local business directory** for Johnson County, KS. The goal is to generate **organic search traffic** from people searching for home services in JoCo cities (Overland Park, Olathe, Lenexa, etc.) and **convert that traffic into revenue**.

## SEO Strategy

### The Core Play: Local Service Keywords

Every page on the site targets a specific local search intent:
- "HVAC Overland Park" → `/hvac/overland-park`
- "Plumber Olathe KS" → `/plumbing/olathe`
- "Roofing contractor Lenexa" → `/roofing/lenexa`

These are **high-intent, low-competition** keywords. Someone searching "HVAC repair Overland Park" needs HVAC repair *now* — they're not browsing. The 16 categories × 9 cities = 144 page combinations, each targeting a specific keyword cluster.

### What Makes Local Directories Rank

1. **Unique content on every page** — Not just business listings. Each page needs:
   - A unique intro paragraph (category + city specific)
   - Unique FAQs
   - Unique meta description and title
   - Unique H1 and H2 structure
   
2. **Business detail depth** — The more data per listing (reviews, hours, services, photos), the better. Google rewards pages with rich, structured data.

3. **Schema markup** — LocalBusiness structured data helps Google understand what each listing is and can enable rich snippets (stars in search results).

4. **Internal linking** — Cross-linking between categories and cities creates a strong internal link structure that distributes page authority.

5. **Freshness** — Regular data updates signal to Google that the site is maintained and current.

### What We Should Add

- **Individual business pages** (`/hvac/overland-park/integrity-heating-and-air`) — Each business gets its own page with full details, reviews, and schema markup. This multiplies our page count from 144 to 1,200+ and targets long-tail searches like "Integrity Heating Merriam reviews."

- **Review aggregation** — Pull Google/Yelp ratings and display them with schema markup. Star ratings in search results dramatically increase click-through.

- **City landing pages** — Each city page (`/cities/overland-park`) aggregates all categories, targeting "Overland Park home services" type searches.

- **Blog/content** — "Best HVAC companies in Overland Park 2025", "How to choose a plumber in Olathe", etc. These target informational searches and drive top-of-funnel traffic.

- **Schema.org markup** — Add `LocalBusiness`, `AggregateRating`, and `FAQPage` structured data to every page.

## Monetization

### Phase 1: Free Listings (Current)
- All businesses listed for free
- Build traffic and domain authority first
- No monetization yet — focus on content and SEO

### Phase 2: Premium Listings ($49-99/month)
- Enhanced listing: logo, longer description, featured badge, photos
- Priority placement above free listings (marked "Sponsored" or "Featured")
- Direct link to their website (free listings get a nofollow link)
- This is the **Yelp/Angi model** — free basic listing, paid premium listing

### Phase 3: Lead Generation ($20-50 per lead)
- "Get a Quote" buttons that send leads to businesses
- Charge per qualified lead (name, phone, service needed)
- Higher value per transaction than premium listings
- Requires lead tracking infrastructure

### Phase 4: Advertising
- Google AdSense as baseline revenue (low but passive)
- Direct ad sales to businesses in competitive categories (HVAC, plumbing, roofing)
- Category sponsors ($200-500/month for exclusive placement)

## Competitive Positioning

### Why This Can Work
- **Yelp** charges businesses $229/month and is increasingly resented for aggressive sales tactics
- **Angi** (HomeAdvisor) sells leads to multiple contractors, reducing quality
- **Google Business Profile** — free but not a directory; businesses still need third-party visibility
- **Thumbtack** — lead generation model, not a directory

The gap: there's no **free, simple, local-specific** directory for JoCo. "JoCo Home Pros" targets a specific geographic niche that national directories can't match for local SEO.

### Why JoCo Specifically
- Johnson County is affluent (median household income $96K)
- High homeownership rate (~70%)
- Growing population (600K+)
- Dense cluster of cities = lots of cross-city searches
- "JoCo" is a recognized local term with search volume

## Growth Priorities (Ranked)

1. **Complete data coverage** — Make sure every category has 20+ listings per city. The v2 scraper with service areas is a huge step.

2. **Individual business pages** — Biggest SEO multiplier. 1,200+ pages targeting business name + city searches.

3. **Schema markup** — LocalBusiness + AggregateRating + FAQPage on every page.

4. **Unique content per page** — Don't just list businesses. Add city+category specific intro text, FAQs, and buying guides.

5. **Google Business Profile links** — Link to each business's GBP page. This creates a connection that Google can verify.

6. **Premium listing outreach** — Once we have traffic (even 100 visits/day), start contacting businesses.

7. **Lead generation** — After premium listings are established, add quote request forms.
